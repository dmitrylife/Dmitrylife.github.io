About Ψ-Theory

Overview

The Psi-Continuum is a developing theoretical framework exploring the idea that the structure of space-time, gravitational behaviour, and large-scale energy flows may emerge from deeper phase-like properties of the cosmic medium.

This page provides a high-level conceptual outline. The technical development, equations, and cosmological analysis will be made available after the release of the corresponding arXiv preprint.

Core Ideas

  • Space-time as a phase-structured continuum
  • Gravity emerging from phase gradients and energy redistribution
  • Non-equilibrium contributions to the late-time cosmic expansion
