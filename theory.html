About Ψ-Theory

Overview

The Psi-Continuum is a developing theoretical framework exploring the idea that the structure of space-time, gravitational behaviour, and large-scale energy flows may emerge from deeper phase-like properties of the cosmic medium.

This page will provide a general conceptual plan. The technical development, equations, and cosmological analysis will be made available after the release of the corresponding preprint.

Publication Status

The Psi-Continuum framework now consists of two complementary research releases: an initial phenomenological preprint and the expanded Version 2 with full ΛCDM/ΨCDM comparison, DESI DR2 inclusion, and reproducible computational tools.
