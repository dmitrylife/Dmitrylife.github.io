About Me

I am an independent researcher from Kaliningrad (Königsberg) working on late–time cosmology and theoretical aspects of gravitational physics. My main focus is the development of the Psi-Continuum framework — a field-based approach that explores whether certain gravitational and cosmological phenomena can emerge from deeper phase-like properties of the cosmic medium. My work aims to remain strictly within the standards of reproducible scientific methodology: all numerical tests, cosmological likelihoods, and datasets are fully open-source and will continue to be expanded.

My primary work is the development of the Psi-Continuum Theory — a field-based model describing gravity, energy flow, and the phase structure of reality.

Research Topics

  • phenomenological in late-time cosmology
  • background expansion tests and model comparison
  • regularization mechanisms for high-density regimes
  • future extension toward perturbations and field dynamics
