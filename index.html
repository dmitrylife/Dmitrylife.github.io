About Me

My name is Klimov Dmitry Vasilevich. I am an independent theoretical researcher from Kaliningrad / Königsberg with a long-standing interest in physics, energy systems, field theory, and the foundations of space-time.

I studied Theoretical Physics at the Baltic Federal University (formerly KGU) from 2002 to 2004. Although I did not complete the degree, physics has remained my core passion throughout my entire life.

I am also the General Director of two engineering companies working in heating systems and thermal engineering, but my main intellectual work focuses on the development of the Psi-Continuum Theory.

  • Energy-phase curvature of space-time
