About Ψ-Continuum Preprints arXiv

Submission Context

The Psi-Continuum framework is an independent research program focused on late-time cosmology and non-equilibrium macroscopic response.

The work is prepared for submission to arXiv in the category: hep-ph (phenomenology of high-energy physics).

The project includes:

  • peer-style structured preprints (v2, v5)
  • fully reproducible computational pipelines
  • open datasets and likelihood implementations (SN, H(z), BAO, DESI)
  • public documentation and versioned releases
